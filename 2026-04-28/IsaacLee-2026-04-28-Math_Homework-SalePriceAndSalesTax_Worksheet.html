Sale Price and Total Cost with Sales Tax

Student: Isaac Lee Date: 2026-04-28
Standard: CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.6.RP.A.3.C / 7.RP.A.3 — Find a percent of a quantity; solve real-world problems involving discounts (markdowns) and sales tax (markups).
Quick Reference
  • Sale Price (% off): Sale Price = Original Price − (Percent × Original Price)
  • Total Cost with Sales Tax: Total Cost = Original Price + (Tax Percent × Original Price)
  • Convert percent to decimal: divide by 100. (Example: 25% = 0.25 = \(\tfrac{1}{4}\))
  • Show your work — write the percent calculation, then the final price.
