Sale Price and Total Cost with Sales Tax

Student: Isaac Lee Date: 2026-04-28
Standard: CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.6.RP.A.3.C / 7.RP.A.3 — Percent of a quantity; discounts and sales tax.
Part A — Find the Sale Price
Problem 1
A jacket originally costs $80 and is on sale for 25% off. Find the sale price.
Discount 25% = 1/4
Discount amount: \(0.25 \times \$80 = \$20\).
Sale price: \(\$80 - \$20 = \$60\).
$60
Problem 2

Discount

$40

Part B — Find the Total Cost with Sales Tax
Problem 11
A T-shirt costs $20, and there is a 5% sales tax. Find the total cost.
Sales Tax
Tax amount: \(0.05 \times \$20 = \$1\).
Total cost: \(\$20 + \$1 = \$21\).
$21
