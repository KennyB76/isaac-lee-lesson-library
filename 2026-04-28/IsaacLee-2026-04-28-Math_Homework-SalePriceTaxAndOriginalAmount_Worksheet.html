Quick Reference — Forward and Reverse
  • Forward (find the new price): Sale Price = Original × (1 − rate);   Total Cost = Original × (1 + rate)
  • Reverse (find the original): Original = Sale Price ÷ (1 − rate);   Original = Total Cost ÷ (1 + rate)
  • Find the rate: rate = (change ÷ original) × 100%
  • Multi-step: Apply the discount FIRST, then add sales tax to the discounted price.
