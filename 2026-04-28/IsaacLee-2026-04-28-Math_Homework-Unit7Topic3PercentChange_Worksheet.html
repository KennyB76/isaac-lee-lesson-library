Quick Reference — From Today's Lecture
  • Percent of Change formula: \(\dfrac{\text{Amount of Change}}{\text{Original Amount}} = \dfrac{X}{100}\)
  • Sales Tax: Tax = Rate × Marked Price;   Total Cost = Marked Price + Tax
  • Discount: Discount = Rate × List Price;   Sale Price = List Price − Discount
  • Markup (cost → selling price): Markup % = (Selling Price − Cost) ÷ Cost × 100%
  • Reverse: Original = Sale Price ÷ (1 − rate);   Original = Total ÷ (1 + rate)
