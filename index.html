"Isaac — This week we're seeing area instead of memorizing formulas. 1 m² is just a 1m × 1m little square. A parallelogram is a rectangle in disguise. A trapezoid is half of a parallelogram you build from two flipped copies. Play with the Area Explorer to convince yourself — then race the clock on the Perimeter game. The equation always comes after the picture. — Mr. K"
