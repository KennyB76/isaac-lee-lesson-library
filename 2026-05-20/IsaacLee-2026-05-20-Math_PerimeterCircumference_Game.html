⚡ The Rules
  • Perimeter — total distance around the outside. Add every side.
  • Area — number of unit squares inside. Use the right formula for each shape. Answer is in squared units (m², cm² …).
  • Circle: $C = \pi \cdot d$, $\;A = \pi \cdot r^2$  (use $\pi = 3.14$).
  • Type the answer, press Enter or Submit. Tolerance ±0.05.
