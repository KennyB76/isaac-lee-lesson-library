Problem 18 — Trapezoid b₁=7, b₂=3⅓, h=4⅔ (mm)
Mixed-number trapezoid
Convert $b_2 = \dfrac{10}{3}$, $\; h = \dfrac{14}{3}$, $\; b_1 = 7 = \dfrac{21}{3}$
Sum $b_1 + b_2 = \dfrac{21}{3} + \dfrac{10}{3} = \dfrac{31}{3}$
Times h $\dfrac{31}{3} \times \dfrac{14}{3} = \dfrac{434}{9}$
Half $\dfrac{434}{9} \div 2 = \dfrac{434}{18} = \dfrac{217}{9} = 24\tfrac{1}{9}$
24 1/9 mm²
Part E — Word Problems
Problem 19 — Trapezoid: area = 96 cm², h = 6 cm, b₂ = b₁ + 4. Find b₁, b₂.
Reverse the formula
Setup $\dfrac{(b_1 + b_2) \times 6}{2} = 96 \Rightarrow (b_1 + b_2) \times 3 = 96$
Solve $b_1 + b_2 = 32$
Substitute $b_1 + (b_1 + 4) = 32 \Rightarrow 2b_1 = 28 \Rightarrow b_1 = 14$
Therefore $b_2 = 14 + 4 = 18$
b₁ = 14 cm, b₂ = 18 cm
